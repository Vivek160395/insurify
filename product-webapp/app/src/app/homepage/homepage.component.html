How to buy an Insurance Online

Even though the process of buying an insurance online is simple, different people can have a varied set of doubts.Here's what you need to do :

1. Visit the insurance page of our website

2. In the next step you can select the Insurance type and policy type of your choice.

3. Enter all the necessary details like your mobile no, email ID, Aadhar card number etc.

4. After the main selection, you can choose to edit your coverage as much as the insurance provider allows by selecting the right add-ons and giving details about persons you would like to be covered etc.

5. When your selection is finished, you can make a payment through debit cards, credit cards, net-banking, etc.

6. You will then receive your acknowledgement through email.

Robust Claim Settlement Process
